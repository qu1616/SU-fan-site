Episodes

Ever since Steven Universe aired, it's been considered one of the highest rated television shows on Cartoon Network. Airing a whopping number of 160 episodes; it's clear that the series has been very successful. In addition, after the recent release of Steven Universe: The Movie, a new epilogue series (basically season 6) called Steven Universe Future was announced for 2020. The Crewniverse will be producing new episodes with this series! (Links are provided with episode descriptions)

Movie

The Steven Universe Movie follows the events of Steven and the Crystal Gems 2 years after the end of season 5 (change your mind). Beach City is finally at peace after fighting against the Diamonds for the colonization of Earth. However, Steven's "happily ever after" is put to a hold once a mysterious Gem arrives on Earth; looking for Steve and the rest of the Crystal Gems.
