Music

Steven Universe is known for its various musical works throughout the entire series. With the help of the Crewniverse, Rebecca Sugar creates original songs meant specifically for Steven Universe. These songs are always a big part of the development of a specific character. Unfortunately, a song is not featured in every episode. The movie however, was filled with a number of songs making it a "musical" movie. Fun fact: when hiring the voices for the characters, one of the requirements Rebecca Sugar had was for the person to be a strong singer or at least have some experience.
